  • Successfully completing the first month of the internship is a prerequisite for eligibility.

  • If you choose not to participate in the interview process, you have the option to pay an administrative cost of 149/-.
  • Paying this fee will grant you an Internship Completion Certificate.
  • These certificates will confirm the successful completion of your internship.
